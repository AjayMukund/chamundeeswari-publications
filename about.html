About Chamundeeswari Publications

Chamundeeswari Publications is dedicated to creating beautifully illustrated, educationally enriching books for young children. We believe that the earliest reading experiences shape a child's relationship with knowledge for life.

Our catalogue focuses on phonics-based early readers, designed for children aged 3–6, combining vibrant illustrations with carefully crafted text to build vocabulary, comprehension, and a genuine love of reading.

Every book we publish is developed with the same rigour and care that defines our founding team — rooted in a tradition of precision, clarity, and integrity.
