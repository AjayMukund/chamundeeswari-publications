Because our products are digital files that are delivered immediately upon payment, we do not offer refunds once the download link has been sent to your email address. This policy is consistent with standard practice for digital goods under Indian consumer protection guidelines.

Exceptions

We will replace or re-deliver your purchase at no charge if:

  • The PDF file you received is corrupted, incomplete, or unreadable

To report either of these issues, please email us within 7 days of your purchase date with your order reference number (included in the Instamojo confirmation email).

Orders cannot be cancelled once payment has been confirmed, as digital delivery is immediate and automatic. If you have accidentally placed a duplicate order for the same title, please contact us and we will review the case.

Chamundeeswari Publications sells digital products only. We do not sell or ship physical books. There are no physical goods, no packaging, and no courier or postal delivery involved in any transaction.

How Delivery Works

Upon successful payment, our payment partner Instamojo will automatically send a secure download link to the email address you provided at checkout, typically within a few minutes of payment confirmation.
